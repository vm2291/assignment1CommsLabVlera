The Art of Noticing:

A Closer Look at NYUAD Campus

Beauty lives in the details, most people walk by, but those who notice find a world of wonder.




The Inspiration

Every frame in this movie is a tribute to the unnoticed wonders of campus life. Here are some of the moments and elements that inspired the story.

Two birds meet, chirping and sharing a moment. Their small interactions bring life to the grass and remind us to notice the simple joys around us.

A close look at the tree reveals patterns like living art. Every twist and texture tells a story of time, growth, and hidden beauty.

A gentle dog, fur and breath moving softly in the sun. In these quiet moments, we see warmth, comfort, and the peace of being present.

The Movie

"The Art of Noticing: A Closer Look at NYUAD Campus" is a short movie that invites viewers to slow down and appreciate the beauty in everyday moments. Through zoomed-in shots of flowers, leaves, birds, and more, the film reveals the hidden artistry all around us.
